✦ Our Story ✦

Where Tradition
Meets Craft

Pratibha Heritage Craft was born from a deep love for the ancient art of handknitting and weaving. We believe that every handmade piece carries a soul — the warmth of the artisan's hands, the patience of the process, the beauty of imperfection.

Our creations are made with carefully sourced natural yarns and fibres, honouring techniques passed down through generations. From cozy knitted shawls to intricate decorative pieces — each item is a celebration of heritage.

  • 100% handcrafted — no machine replication
  • Natural, premium-quality yarns & fibres
  • Custom orders welcomed — made just for you
  • Shipped with care, packed with love
