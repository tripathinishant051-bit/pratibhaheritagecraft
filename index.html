Our Story

Rooted in
Craft, Guided
by Heritage.

Pratibha Heritage Craft was born from a deep love for India's handloom traditions. Founded by Pratibha Tripathi in Prayagraj, every piece we create is a labour of patience, precision, and passion.

We believe that handmade is not just a method — it is a philosophy. Each knit, each weave carries the warmth of human hands and the soul of generations of craft.

From cosy winter wraps to delicate decorative pieces, our collection is crafted to be treasured across lifetimes.
